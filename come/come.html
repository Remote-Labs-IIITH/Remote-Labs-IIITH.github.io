How to use this dashboard

  • Power On Button: Sets off the escalator to carry the ball to its initial position.
  • Open Barriers Button: Opens up te shafts that act as barriers.
  • Reset Button: Resets all parameters to its original placement.
  • Leave Session Button: You can exit to the homepage after finishing the experiment.

  • Note: There's a latency of about 5-10 seconds in video feed.
