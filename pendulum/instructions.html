Remote Triggered Lab

Conservation Of Energy

Aim

To show that sum of potential and kinetic energies of a moving object at various observation points in a track are almost equal.


Apparatus

  • Roller coaster track
  • IR sensors
  • Servo motors
  • Raspberry Pi Microcontroller


Physics of the Experiment

The main physics behind the experiment can be summarized as:

According to the law of conservation of energy, energy can neither be created nor destroyed - only converted from one form to another. Thus, a system always has the same amount of energy unless an external force is applied. The primary two types of energy are:

  • Potential energy (PE)
  • Kinetic energy (KE)

When there are no external forces, $$TE = PE + KE = constant$$ at any point on the track.
