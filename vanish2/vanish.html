How to use this dashboard

  • Use the Up and Down arrows in the control widget to control the position of the glass rods. You can do this multiple times to see the rod vanishing in the left beaker container sunflower oil while it remains clrealy visible in the tight beaker containging warter.
  • You can click the Recalibrate Button to reset the setup to default state. After you are done with the experiment, click the "leave session" button on the top right corner to exit the session. This will redirect you to thr home page.

  • Note: There's a latency of about 5-10 seconds in video feed.
